Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the flight information?

+

AirportBot aggregates information from multiple reliable sources to provide the most accurate information possible. However, for real-time flight status, we always recommend checking with the airline directly.

Is my conversation with AirportBot private?

+

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Your conversations are not shared with third parties, and we only use anonymized data to improve our AI's performance.

Can I use AirportBot offline?

+

Currently, AirportBot requires an internet connection to access its database and provide responses. We're exploring offline capabilities for future updates.

How do I report inaccurate information?

+

If you notice any inaccuracies in our responses, please use the contact form above to report them. Include as much detail as possible to help us improve.
